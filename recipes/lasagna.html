Lasagna

Description

This is a lasagna recipe found on The Cozy Cook A delicious, homemade, classic Italian lasagna with lots of ricotta, mozzerella and a savory meat sauce.

Ingredients

Cheese filling

  • 15 oz. riocotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper

Meat sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • ¾ lb. ground beef
  • ¾ lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese
